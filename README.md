Interactive Knowledge Cards: The Psychology of Money
====================================================

Product Overview
----------------
This is an interactive digital learning product inspired by Morgan Housel’s bestselling book  
**"The Psychology of Money."**  
It transforms 5 core wealth and behavioral finance concepts into engaging, interactive HTML knowledge cards.

Product Value
-------------
1. **Turn Abstract Wealth Principles into Interactive Learning** – Learn through exploration, not passive reading  
2. **Master the Psychology Behind Financial Success** – Understand the mental models that shape long-term wealth  
3. **Practical & Reflective** – Every card connects financial psychology to real-life decision-making  

Target Audience
---------------
- Individual investors seeking better financial decision-making  
- Young professionals building long-term wealth habits  
- Personal finance and self-improvement enthusiasts  
- Coaches, educators, and creators teaching wealth mindset  

Key Features
------------
• 5 Interactive HTML Knowledge Cards  
• Built-in simulations, self-assessments, and reflection exercises  
• No installation required – works in any modern browser  
• Mobile-responsive design  
• Instant digital download  

Cards Included
--------------
1. **No One Is Crazy**  
   Understand why everyone’s financial decisions make sense in the context of their own life experiences  
   *Interactive Feature: Financial Background Decision Simulator*

2. **Getting Rich vs Staying Rich**  
   Learn why making money and keeping money require entirely different skill sets  
   *Interactive Feature: Survival vs Risk Decision Game*

3. **Enough**  
   Discover how unchecked greed destroys wealth and why knowing your limits matters  
   *Interactive Feature: Wealth Desire Threshold Assessment*

4. **Confounding Compounding**  
   Experience how compounding feels slow at first, then becomes exponential over time  
   *Interactive Feature: Dynamic Compound Growth Visualizer*

5. **Freedom Is the Highest Dividend**  
   Realize that the ultimate purpose of wealth is control over your own time  
   *Interactive Feature: Time Freedom Calculator*

How to Use
----------
1. Download and extract the product files  
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser  
3. Click any card to begin exploring interactive content  
4. Complete exercises and simulations for deeper learning  

Technical Requirements
----------------------
• Any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)  
• No internet required after download  
• No software installation needed
